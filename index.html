Doro Wat

Doro Wat is Ethiopia's famous spicy chicken stew simmered with caramelized onions, berbere, and niter kibbeh (spiced butter). It’s rich, comforting, and perfect for sharing with family.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken (drumsticks or thighs), skin removed
  • 4 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp niter kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced butter) or oil
  • 2–3 tbsp berbere spice (to taste)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2½ cups chicken stock
  • 4 boiled eggs, peeled
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook onions on medium heat until golden brown.
  2. Add butter, garlic, and ginger; cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in berbere and tomato paste.
  4. Add chicken and stock, simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Add eggs and salt; simmer 5 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with injera. Enjoy!
