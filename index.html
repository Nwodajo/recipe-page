🍳 My Favorite Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in the egg, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and pour ¼ cup batter for each pancake.
  4. Cook until golden on both sides, then serve warm.

Enjoy your delicious pancakes!
